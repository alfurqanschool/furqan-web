Al-Furqan School: Fostering a Family of Quran Memorizers

Al-Furqan School provides a very unique program in Ottawa, in fact in North America. It is unique in that it takes place within a Canadian context yet has the Quran at its foundation. Many students have graduated over the years with the Quran fully memorized and with academic skills that match or surpass their peers in public schools.
Since its inception, Al-Furqan School has grown steadily from year to year. It started off with a part-time Quran Hifth program, but then in 2011, we embarked on a new project, with just five students, opening a full-time Islamic School that graduates students with the Quran fully memorized over a four-year period.
We now offer both a part-time and full-time program and serve more than 350 students, aged 5 and above, at two separate locations in the city (one in the east end and one in the west). Some of our advanced students have transferred to our full-time elementary school, where they have memorized the entire Holy Quran. A few have even been given ijaza by our highly acclaimed Sheikh Abu Kudus, who himself has 10 qira'at.
